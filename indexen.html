Mission

ASSA SERVICIOS Y MANUFACTURACIÓN S.A. DE C.V. is a competitive and reliable company in the manufacture and service of parts for the automotive industry and the industry in general, to satisfy with quality and timely response to the needs of our customers and the market in general. We are committed to the development of our people, the progress of the community, the care of the environment, and the development of our suppliers.

Vision

To be by 2026 a company with diversified products and services for the automotive industry and the industry in general, complying with the certification requirements, supplying high-quality products that meet the expectations of our customers, and promoting the development of our collaborators, local and regional development through job creation; working together with the community, respecting the environment and taking care of people's safety. Certifications

We are currently ISO 9001: 2015 certified and IATF 16949: 2016 certified.

  • ISO 9001:2015

    It is an international standard that focuses on all the elements of quality management that the company must have to improve the quality of its products and services. This achieving the satisfaction of its customers.

  • IATF 16949:2016

    It's a Quality Management System that provides a continuous improvement, emphasizing the prevention of errors and the reduction of variation and waste in the automotive supply chain.

Facilities

Information about our Facilities

Brief description of our facilities and processes.

  • Facility 1

    It has 15 production lines to manufacture parts according to the specifications of our clients. Our facilities have laboratories equipped to meet the needs of our customers.

  • Laboratory

    ASSA completes different test methods in our laboratory, such as humidity control according to the Client's requirements. The laboratory performs tests with industrial furnaces, precision scales and calibrated measuring devices. All parts are free of moisture above 0.3% and parts are certified by quality auditors. Professional packaging with moisture indicators and desiccants is required to prevent moisture inside the boxes.

  • Facility 2 - Washing process

    Ultrasonic immersion metal parts washing plant. The plant is projected in the first phase with 7 ultrasonic washing equipment.

  • Facility 2 - Cutting Process

    Tube dice cutting station, according to customer specifications.

  • Facility 2 - Forming Process

    We have 2 forming stations, performing the forming process according to our client's specifications.
